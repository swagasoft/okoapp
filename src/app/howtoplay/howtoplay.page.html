HOW TO PLAY I-SABI

Create Account

Click on sign up then input the required information needed I.e phone / username / password/ confirm password. Click submit. NB cross check your details properly before submitting.
Credit Account

Click on the account section. Click on the enter amount free space. First you have to input the amount intended to credit your i-sabi wallet with. Click on the preferred payment model.
I-sabi Live Session

i-sabi live session starts from 6am - 6pm every Saturday, make sure you are logged in to participate.

Each game session cost two hundred naira (200)
Stay Connected

Stay connected when games are on. Make sure that your network or wifi is connected properly so you do not get interrupted during game play.

How Much Do You Know?

Endeavour to answer 15 question correctly under 4 minutes and you stand the chance of winning 5k cash price.

I-sabi Cash Prize

Top five winners on our leaderboard get paid a sum of five thousand naira (5,000) after every Saturday game play.

Clue Tips

Clue tip gives insight to the questions for next game to be played. They role from 6:02pm Saturday to the next week Saturday by 5:59am.

Leaderboard

The leaderboard displays everyone that answers their questions correctly. The top five players will be rewarded as the winners for the week.
